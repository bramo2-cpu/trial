Hibiscus

Hibiscus plants are prized for their large, colorful, trumpet-shaped flowers. They thrive in warm climates like Nairobi and come in various species and cultivars.

Care Tips:

  • Sunlight: Full sun to partial shade.
  • Watering: Regular watering, especially when flowering.
  • Soil: Well-draining, fertile soil.

Succulents

Succulents are water-wise plants perfect for drier areas of Nairobi. They come in fascinating shapes and textures.

Care Tips:

  • Sunlight: Bright, indirect sunlight.
  • Watering: Water sparingly, allowing the soil to dry out completely between waterings.
  • Soil: Well-draining succulent or cactus mix.
